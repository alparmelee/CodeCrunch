(10 pts) Encrypt the plaintext "ITSTIMEFORTHEWEEKEND" using the Playfair cipher and the keyword "SUMMERTIME". Please use 'X' as the padding character and please use the box provided below to visualize the key.

Answer: AITDAUUHQMBDUYMYRHUPGV

The Playfair matrix is constructed from the keyword "SUMMERTIME" (unique letters: SUMERTI). The matrix (with I/J combined) is: S U M E R T I A B C D F G H K L N O P Q V W X Y Z The plaintext "ITSTIMEFORTHEWEEKEND" is broken into bigrams. The double letter 'EE' is padded with 'X'. The last letter 'D' is also padded with an 'X'. Plaintext bigrams: IT, ST, IM, EF, OR, TH, EW, EX, EK, EN, DX. Encryption rules and steps produce the ciphertext shown above.
